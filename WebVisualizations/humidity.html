Humidity



Humidity is in simple terms the amount of water vapor in the air. Humidity, from a weather prespective, is a relative humidity which decreases as air temperature increases since air can hold more water molecules, However, in the data of the cities on hand, there appears to be no relation between the latitude and humidity even though the temperature is warmer near the equator.
